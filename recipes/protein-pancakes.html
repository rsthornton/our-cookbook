Protein Pancakes

Emily's high-protein pancake mix with cottage cheese and collagen

Prep: 10 min Cook: 15 min Total: 25 min Servings: 2
protein breakfast pancakes
+ Add photo

Emily's Version

  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • 4 tablespoons collagen
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 egg white
  • ¾ cup cottage cheese
  • ¼ cup Greek yogurt
  • ½ cup gluten-free pancake mix
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Mix all dry ingredients together (protein powder, collagen, pancake mix, cinnamon)
  2. In a separate bowl, blend cottage cheese, yogurt, eggs, egg white, and vanilla until smooth
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients, stir until just mixed
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat
  5. Pour ¼ cup batter per pancake
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip
  7. Cook another 1-2 minutes until golden
