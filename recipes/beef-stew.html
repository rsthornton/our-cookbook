Beef Stew + Vegetables

Hearty African-inspired beef stew with kale and peanut butter

Prep: 20 min Cook: ~2 hours Total: ~2.5 hours Servings: 6
beef stew hearty
+ Add photo

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs beef, cut into medium-sized cubes
  • 3 large roma tomatoes, chopped
  • 1.5 large onions, chopped
  • 2-3 cubes chicken bouillon
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 habanero pepper
  • 1 packet frozen kale (or 8 packs fresh baby kale)
  • 3 tablespoons smooth peanut butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Part 1: Cooking the Beef (~1.5 hours)

  1. Put beef in a large pot
  2. Pour enough water to cover beef plus 1-2 inches
  3. Turn heat on, add 2-3 cubes chicken bouillon
  4. Add a few dashes of pepper
  5. Let cook, checking every 30 minutes until it reaches a rapid boil
  6. Stir once every 30 minutes
  7. Cook until water is almost gone
  8. Once it starts thickening, add 3 cloves garlic and 1 habanero

Part 2: Preparing Vegetables (~30 minutes)

  1. Add frozen kale (or fresh baby kale) to the pot
  2. Add ½ onion
  3. Add 1 chopped tomato
  4. Add 3 tablespoons smooth peanut butter to the top
  5. Add salt to taste
  6. Once boiling and melted, reduce to a simmer for 30 minutes
  7. Stir frequently
