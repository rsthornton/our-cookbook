Ultimate Protein Pancakes

111g protein per batch — whipped egg whites for maximum fluff

Prep: 15 min Cook: 15 min Total: 30 min Servings: 2
protein breakfast gluten-free
+ Add photo

Ingredients

  • 2 scoops protein powder
  • 1 whole egg
  • 6 egg whites (whip to stiff glossy peaks)
  • 1 cup non-fat Greek yogurt
  • ¼ cup Pamela's GF pancake mix
  • 1 tbsp ground chia seeds
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 3 tbsp vanilla
  • Cinnamon to taste

Instructions

  1. Whip 6 egg whites to stiff, glossy peaks — stop before they look dry or clumpy
  2. In a separate bowl, mix protein powder, pancake mix, ground chia seeds, baking soda, and cinnamon
  3. Add the whole egg, Greek yogurt, and vanilla to the dry ingredients and stir until combined
  4. Fold ⅓ of the whipped egg whites into the batter to lighten it
  5. Gently fold in the remaining egg whites — don't overmix, keep it fluffy
  6. Let batter rest 2-3 minutes so chia can absorb and gel
  7. If batter is too thick, add a splash of water or almond milk
  8. Heat a non-stick pan over medium-low heat — high protein batters burn faster
  9. Pour ¼ cup batter per pancake, cook until edges set and bubbles form
  10. Flip gently, cook another 1-2 minutes until golden

Macros

Full batch: ~782 cal | ~111g protein | ~38g carbs | ~11g fat

Per serving (2 servings): ~391 cal | ~56g protein | ~19g carbs | ~5.5g fat

Notes

The baking soda + whipped egg whites do the heavy lifting for fluffiness. This is the evolved version of Emily's original protein pancakes — the "try next time" ideas all rolled in.
