Chocolate Chia Pudding

5-ingredient overnight chocolate pudding — healthy and easy

Prep: 5 min Cook: None (refrigerate 2+ hours) Total: 2+ hours (mostly waiting) Servings: 1
healthy no-cook chocolate
+ Add photo

Ingredients

  • 150 ml warm milk (any type)
  • 2 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tsp honey or sweetener of your choice
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • Optional topping: berries

Instructions

  1. Blend everything well
  2. Pour into a glass or jar
  3. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours (overnight is best)
  4. Top with berries if desired

Notes

This is basically dessert that's actually good for you. The chia seeds expand and create a pudding-like texture. Make it the night before for an easy grab-and-go breakfast!
