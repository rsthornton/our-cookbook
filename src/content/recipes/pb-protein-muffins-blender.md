## Ingredients

- 3 ripe bananas (about 1 1/2 heaping cups mashed)
- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup Greek yogurt
- 1/4 cup peanut butter
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/4 cup powdered peanut butter
- 3 scoops whey protein powder (just shy of 1 cup)
- 1 1/4 cups oats
- Chocolate chips for topping

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to **350°F**
2. Combine all ingredients in blender except chocolate chips — blend well
3. If using plant-based protein powder, add more liquid until consistency is like honey
4. Grease or spray muffin pan
5. Pour batter evenly into pan, top each with a few chocolate chips
6. Bake **22-24 minutes** until tops and sides start to brown

## Notes

- Blender method = no bowls to wash
- Plant-based protein absorbs more liquid — adjust consistency
