## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
- 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 cup protein powder
- 1/2 cup raw honey
- 3 very ripe bananas, mashed
- 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
- 1/2 cup natural creamy peanut butter

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to **375°F**
2. Spray muffin tin with cooking spray or line with liners
3. Mix flour, baking powder, salt, and protein powder in a medium bowl
4. Add honey, mashed bananas, and applesauce — stir until combined
5. Add peanut butter and mix again
6. Bake **25-30 minutes** until edges are golden brown and toothpick comes out clean

## Notes

- Fridge: airtight container, up to 5 days
- Freezer: airtight container, up to 3 months
