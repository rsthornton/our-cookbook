## Ingredients

- 2 lbs ground turkey
- 2+ tbsp Siete spicy taco seasoning (add more to taste)
- 2 cups spinach
- 1/4 cup broth
- 1 tsp ghee
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Heat pan with **ghee** over medium-high heat
2. Add ground turkey and brown as best you can, breaking it up as it cooks
3. Add **taco seasoning**, salt, and pepper, mix well
4. Add **spinach** and cook until wilted
5. Pour in **1/4 cup broth** and let it cook down

## Notes

Serve in tortillas with your favorite toppings. Adjust seasoning to taste — start with 2 tbsp and add more as needed.
