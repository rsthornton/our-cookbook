## Version 1: Optimized (79 cal / 11g protein per bite)

- 4 medium eggs
- 6 egg whites
- 1 cup low-fat cottage cheese (28g protein)
- 3 cups spinach
- 12 tsp Parmesan (~4 tbsp)
- Green onion
- 9 slices turkey bacon

**Full batch:** ~943 cal / ~130g protein
**Per bite (÷12):** ~79 cal / ~11g protein

## Version 2: Max Protein (~78 cal / ~12g protein per bite)

- 3 medium eggs
- 7 egg whites
- 1 cup fat-free cottage cheese (28g protein)
- 3 cups spinach
- 12 tsp Parmesan (~4 tbsp)
- Green onion
- 11 slices turkey bacon
- 1 scoop collagen peptides

**Full batch:** ~930 cal / ~147g protein
**Per bite (÷12):** ~78 cal / ~12g protein

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F
2. Place a separate oven-safe pan of water on the lower rack to create steam (keeps the bites creamy without the mess of a water bath)
3. Grease a 12-cup muffin tin well (or use silicone molds)
4. Cook and crumble the turkey bacon
5. Blend cottage cheese, spinach, green onion (and collagen if using) until smooth
6. Hand whisk the whole eggs and egg whites together, then stir into the blended mixture until fully combined
7. Divide Parmesan and crumbled turkey bacon directly into the cups
8. Pour egg mixture evenly over the Parmesan and bacon in each cup
9. Bake **25-30 minutes** until set and firm to the touch
10. Let cool 5 minutes before removing

## Storage

- **Fridge:** 4-5 days in an airtight container
- **Freezer:** Up to 2 months — reheat from frozen ~90 sec in the microwave
