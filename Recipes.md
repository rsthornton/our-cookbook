# Beef Stew \+ Vegetables Recipe

1. Chop ingredients  
   1. Chop 3 large tomatoes (roma)  
   2. Chop 1 large onions  
   3. 3lbs beef cut up into medium sized cubes  
   4. Chicken bouillon  
2. Cooking beef (\~1.5 hours)  
   1. Put beef in pot  
   2. Pour enough water to cover beef \+ 1-2 inches  
   3. Turn heat on  
   4. Add 2-3 cubes chicken bouillon  
   5. Add few dashes pepper   
   6. Let cook check every 30 minutes, until rapid boil  
   7. Stir once every 30 minutes  
   8. Cook until water almost gone  
   9. Once starts thickening, add (3 cloves) garlic and 1 habenero  
3. Preparing vegetables (\~30 minutes)  
   1. Add 1 packet frozen kale (8 packs fresh baby kale) to pot  
   2. Add ½ onion  
   3. Add 1 chopped tomato   
   4. Add 3 tablespoons smooth peanut butter to top  
   5. Add salt to top  
   6. Once boiling/cooked/melted reduce to simmer for 30 minutes  
   7. Stir frequently
